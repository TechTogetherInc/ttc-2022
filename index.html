Come travel to the future at TechTogether Chicago’s first gender-focused hackathon!

Let's Hack the Future! Welcome to TechTogether Chicago, which is going to be held virtuall from February 25 - 27, 2022! Our mission here in Chicago is to empower gender-marginalized individuals in hackathon environments!
